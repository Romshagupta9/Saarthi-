Aptitude Questions

Q2: Solve: 20% of 600 + 30% of 500.
Q3: A car travels 450 km in 9 hours. Find its average speed.
Q4: Probability of drawing a red card from a deck of cards.
Q5: Find the compound interest on $8000 at 6% for 3 years.
Q6: Find the HCF of 56, 98, and 112.
Q7: Solve for x: 3x + 5 = 20.
Q8: Find the sum of the first 15 odd numbers.
Q9: A train moves at 80 km/hr for 3 hours. Find the distance covered.
Q10: Find the LCM of 24, 36, and 60.
Q11: Solve: (12 + 6) × (15 ÷ 3).
Q12: Find the median of the numbers: 1, 5, 7, 3, 9.
Q13: A person spends 35% of his salary. Find his savings if salary is $4000.
Q14: Calculate the total cost if 8 items cost $15 each.
Q15: Solve: (16² - 8²) ÷ 8.
